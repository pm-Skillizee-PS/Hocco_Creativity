ANKIT CHONA

INDIA LOVES ITS ICE CREAM!

India is one of the largest markets for ice cream in the world. We have the biggest dairy industry that feeds into a large ice cream industry as well. It is also a competitive category dominated by legacy players with decades-long regional strongholds. However, our per capita consumption of ice cream remains relatively low compared to other countries. Theres plenty of room for growth.

While there have been a lot of homechefs and boutique brands that have emerged in metros with innovations and premium offerings, there are few brands who have achieved significant scale in the mass category.

The way to unlock ice creams in India is through really good distribution, affordable prices and really good quality ice cream - which is often a tough task for new players. As a result, it has been years since a new mass Indian ice cream brand has been successfully launched.

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE RESEARCH

A majority of ice cream in India is sold in our bustling, crowded markets, and general trade stores. Picture this packed freezers and crowded retail environments where every brand is vying for attention.

To match Hoccos business ambitions, the branding too, needed to be effective and disruptive in this space. This would need a simplicity of thought that could be easily communicated. A distinctive yet flexible brand world that could be translated to a plethora of use cases and an inviting vibe that appealed to people across demographics.
