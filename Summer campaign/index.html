Summer Campaign

OOH

If you're familiar with the ice cream market, Gujarat has one of the highest sales in the country and happens to be the state in which Hocco is well-known and has a prominent presence in retail. With the rebranding of Hocco and the launch of their own wide range of products in company-owned retail outlets as well as general trade, they aimed to create a visible mark through their outdoor campaign.

This was an opportunity to explore what summer would look like in the Hoccoverse. The campaign aimed to build market presence and increase the popularity of the mascots. The visual differentiation and freshness of the campaign set it apart from other brands that had similar prominence in out-of-home ads across the state.

IN THE PRESS

The ask was to create a clutter-breaking campaign that featured our mascots and reimagined summer - one that was set in the Hoccoverse. In the words of the founder, the campaign “Hit it out of the park!”

THE IDEA

Hooked on Hocco

When the mercury climbs, we find ourselves fantasizing about dramatic ways to cool off. ‘Hooked on Hocco’ celebrates the ice cream obsession that summer brings. One can never have enough - more flavours, formats, moments of consumption. We imagine these ways through the eyes of our mascots.

A GLIMPSE INTO THE ILLUSTRATION PROCESS

We took inspiration from our own memories of summer and all the ways we wanted to escape the summer heat. These were reimagined as whimsical scenarios wherein Hocco ice creams became the medium that brought the chill to the mascots in the Hoccoverse.

OOH ADVERTISEMENT

As a campaign, ‘Hooked on Hocco’ was quite a departure from the way traditional brands advertise in the summer and stood out for all the right reasons.
