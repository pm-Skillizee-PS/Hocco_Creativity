Hocco's Story - From Scoop to Success
Discover how a Legacy started Hocco in 2023. Following a 5-year non-compete clause, the Chona family (Satish, Pradeep, and Ankit) launched Hocco in 2023 with bold, premium branding that balances tradition with modernity.
Hocco embraces unique Indian flavors like Aamchi and Charcoal Lychee, creating an emotional connection with customers through quality ingredients and minimal advertising.
